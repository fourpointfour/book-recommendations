## Why I recommend this book

I started reading this because a friend lent me her copy of the book and forced me to read it and I swear, I was hooked since the beginning. I do not have enough words to describe what this book made me feel and I am so glad this book and this story found me.

> I recommend this because this is my favorite book after the Harry Potter series.

### Favorite ideas

1. This is a story as narrated by death, and when death tells you a story, you really have to listen.
