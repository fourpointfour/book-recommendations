## Why I recommend this book

This is definitely a refreshing tale about Ramayana from Sita's perspective. Something we haven't seen or heard from anyone growing up. Whatever we heard was always from either Rama's perspective or someone's perspective who was in search of Sita but nobody ever talked about what could have been going on with Sita.

I recommend this because sometimes to understand an epic, you need different perspectives and not the ones you have been stuck through ages.
