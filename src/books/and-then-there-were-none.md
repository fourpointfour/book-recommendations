## Why I recommend this book

This is the first Agatha Christie book I have ever read and to be frank, this has me hooked for the whole duration of the book. This book's reading journey has spanned an airport, a train, a friend's living room and all I know is that I had no idea what was happening around me for the duration while I was reading this book.
