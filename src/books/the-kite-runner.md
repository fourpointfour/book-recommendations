## Why I recommend this book

Honestly, this was the second Khaled Hosseini book I read in my life and I am still trying to bring myself out of the emotions that I experienced while reading this. The plot will take you places, it will be heartwarming, sad, exciting, happy, gloomy, cheerful - everything at the same time. You just have to be there to witness it.
