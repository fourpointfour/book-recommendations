## Why I recommend this book

First of all, I am a big fan of the Bharat Series by Ashwin Sanghi. The books are not related with each other but every single one of them has a great story to tell.

You'd learn to appreciate how beautifully facts are woven with fiction and mythology while going through this book.

But to be very fair, I rated this 4 because at one point I felt that the book went too deep into the Zoroastrian history which was probably not much relevant to the story and it felt a little out of place in the book because it broke the flow of the story for me at a lot of places. 

### Favorite ideas

1. The links between history and present times.
2. The portrayal of India and it's connection to other events in history.
