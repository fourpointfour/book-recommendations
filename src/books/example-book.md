## Why I recommend this book

Deep Work helped me see how valuable long, uninterrupted focus can be.

- Explains the cost of constant distraction  
- Offers concrete strategies for scheduling focused time  
- Shares real-world case studies and experiments

### Favorite ideas

1. **Time blocking** your calendar.
2. Reducing shallow work to create more room for deep work.
3. Treating focus as a trainable skill, not a fixed trait.
